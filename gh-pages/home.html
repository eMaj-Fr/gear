Meet Gear, an endless possibilities front-end Framework.

What is Gear ?

Another front-end framework. But here comes the twist : the production version of Gear contains Sass files. Gear has been designed with flexibility in mind : options, parameters, which means you can truly take control of your design.

Who is it for ?

Gear is for users already using a front-end Framework (Bootstrap, Foundation...), wanting to experiment something new and customize their design. And also more advanced users ready to make their hands dirty with loads of Sass functionnalities.

Why ?

No more little hacks or tweaks. Gear allows you to natively do what you want. Just grab the documentation, and tell Gear what to do according to your design.
